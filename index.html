How to Fry Eggs

  • Heat nonstick skillet over low heat for 5 minutes.
  • While skillet heats, crack egg(s) into small bowls.
  • Add unsalted butter to skillet. Let butter melt and foam. When foam subsides, swirl skillet to distribute fat evenly.
  • Working quickly, position bowls on either side of skillet and add eggs simultaneously.
  • Season eggs with salt and pepper and cover skillet immediately.
  • Set timer for 2 minutes for runny yolks, 2 1/2 minutes for soft but set yolks, and 3 minutes for firmly set yolks. After alloted time, remove lid and check eggs. If necessary, cover and continue cooking to desired doneness. Transfer eggs to plate and serve.
